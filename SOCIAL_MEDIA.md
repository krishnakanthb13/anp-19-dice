## LinkedIn
I'm excited to announce the initial release of the Dice Roller plugin (v0.0.10) for Amplenote! 🎲

Whether you are running a tabletop campaign, randomly selecting items from tables, or just looking for a quick Magic 8-Ball answer, this plugin turns your notes into a powerful randomizer.

**Key Features Include:**
- **Standard & Advanced Math**: Roll simple d20s or evaluate complex formulas like `3d6 + 2d4 * (2^1d10)`.
- **Game-Specific Systems**: Built-in support for Fate/Fudge dice and Fantasy AGE Stunt checks.
- **Table Randomizer**: Pull random values directly from any markdown table in your notes!
- **Oracles & Specialized**: Magic 8-ball answers, Sicherman dice, Poker dice, and more.
- **Audit Trail**: Every roll is automatically logged to a centralized audit note so you never lose track of your results.

Built with modern JavaScript, fully open-source (GPLv3), and optimized for Amplenote. Try it out and bring some randomness to your workspace!

**Title:** I built a comprehensive Dice Roller & Randomizer plugin for Amplenote! (v0.0.10 Release)

**Body:**
Hey r/Amplenote! 

I’m excited to share the initial release of a new plugin I developed: **Dice Roller**! It lets you simulate all sorts of dice rolls and random selections directly within your notes.

**What it does:**
- **Basic & Advanced Rolls**: Roll standard dice with modifiers (keep/drop, explode, unique) or evaluate complex math expressions.
- **Game Mechanics**: Built-in support for Fate/Fudge dice and Fantasy AGE stunt rolls.
- **Table Randomization**: Highlight a markdown table in your note, and the plugin can randomly select items from it!
- **Oracles**: Includes Magic 8-Ball and specialized dice formats (Poker dice, Sicherman).
- **Audit Logging**: Every roll is automatically saved to a dedicated `Dice Results Audit` note, complete with timestamps and parameters. You can easily view or clear this history from the plugin menu.

The plugin is fully open-source and handles offline-sync states gracefully. I would love for you to try it out for your campaigns or decision-making and let me know what you think!
