## Reddit
**Title:** I built a complete suite of Dice, Oracles, and Decision tools for Amplenote (v0.0.13 Update!)

Hey r/Amplenote!

I wanted to share a major update to the Dice plugin (now v0.0.13). While it started as a simple way to roll a d20 in your notes, I realized there's a lot more to randomization and decision-making when you're managing TTRPG campaigns, writing, or just trying to prioritize tasks.

Here's what's new in this release:
- **Tarot Card Draws**: Pull single cards or spreads (like Celtic Cross) with meanings.
- **Decision Matrix**: Objectively evaluate options based on weighted criteria.
- **Weighted Selector**: Pick items where some outcomes are statistically more likely than others.
- **Dice Pools**: Perfect for Shadowrun/WoD, count "hits" against target numbers.
- **Quick Presets**: Save and re-run your favorite dice configurations instantly.

It's all fully local and logs directly to an audit note so you never lose track of a roll. Would love to hear if you use Amplenote for TTRPGs or worldbuilding and how this fits into your workflow!
