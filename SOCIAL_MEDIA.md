## Reddit
**Title:** I built a comprehensive Dice Roller plugin for Amplenote (Now at v0.0.8)!

**Body:**
Hey r/Amplenote! 

I recently developed a new plugin called Dice Roller that lets you simulate all sorts of dice rolls directly within your notes. Over the past few days, I've shipped several iterative updates (up to v0.0.8), and it's now ready for prime time.

**Journey so far:**
- **v0.0.1-v0.0.2**: Got the core dice rolling mechanics up and running.
- **v0.0.3-v0.0.4**: Prevented navigation errors, optimized API usage, and smoothed out table lookups.
- **v0.0.5**: Shipped a highly requested toggle to let you choose whether to navigate to a looked-up note.
- **v0.0.6-v0.0.8**: Polished the repository with a full test suite, JSDoc comments, comprehensive README, and a security policy.

**How it works:**
The plugin supports Basic rolls (`1d20+5`), Magic 8-Ball answers, Advanced/Fudge/Fate dice, and Random table lookups. You can even configure it to log the results directly into your daily jot or an audit note! It's fully open-source and GPL v3 licensed.

Would love for you to try it out and hear your feedback!
