## 2. Script (Simple Walkthrough Style)

### Introduction
Hello! In this video, we'll walk through the Dice plugin for Amplenote. This plugin brings a massive suite of randomization, decision-making, and worldbuilding tools directly into your notes. Let's explore everything it has to offer.

### Basic and Advanced Dice Rolls
We'll start with the basics. Using the `Basic Dice Roller`, you can roll standard polyhedral dice, specify limits, and even keep or drop the highest results. If you need something more complex, use the `Advanced Dice Expressions` feature to evaluate full mathematical formulas, like rolling `3d4 + 5`. 

### Quick Roll Presets
If you find yourself running the same rolls frequently, the `Quick Roll Presets` module allows you to save and name your favorite dice configurations. You can pull them up instantly without having to retype the formula every time.

### Table Randomizers & Weighted Selections
For those managing lists or random encounters, the `Table Randomizer` is incredibly useful. It can pick random rows and columns from any markdown table in your current note. Additionally, the `Weighted Random Selector` lets you provide options where certain outcomes are statistically more likely to happen than others.

### RPG Dice Systems (Pools, Percentile, Fudge)
If you play tabletop games, you're covered. 
- The `Dice Pool System` lets you roll a handful of dice and count "hits" against a target number—perfect for games like Shadowrun.
- `Percentile Dice` handles d100 rolls, showing you the tens and units digits.
- We also have dedicated support for `Fudge and Fate Dice`, automatically totaling the pluses, minuses, and blanks.

### Generators and Oracles (Tarot, Names, Sai Baba)
The plugin also includes several creative generators. 
- The `Name Generator` provides thematic names for fantasy or sci-fi characters, with optional titles.
- The `Tarot Card Draw` feature lets you draw from the Major Arcana, supporting single card pulls or full Celtic Cross spreads, complete with meanings.
- And for simple questions, you can consult the `Ask Sai Baba` or `Magic 8-Ball` oracles for immediate guidance.

### Decision Matrix
Finally, when you have a tough choice to make, the `Decision Matrix` helps you evaluate multiple options against weighted criteria, giving you an objective, data-driven score for each choice right inside your note.

### Conclusion
Every roll and decision is securely logged to a dedicated Audit Note, so you never lose track of your history. That covers the features of the Dice plugin. Install it today to bring powerful randomization tools to your Amplenote workspace! Thanks for watching.
